// Features

🌀
7 Fractal Types
Mandelbulb · Mandelbox · Torus Knot · Apollonian · Julia 3D · Sierpinski Tetra · Menger Sponge
🎨
9 Color Palettes
Blade Runner · Cyber · Neon Trout · Heat · Frost · Monochrome · Neon Blue · Magenta Cyan · CGA Retro
⚡
Real-Time GPU
Full WebGL 1.0 fragment shader pipeline. Ray marching with 150 steps, normal estimation, Fresnel, specular, and post-processing — all on the GPU.
📦
Zero Dependencies
The entire explorer is a single self-contained HTML file. No npm, no bundler, no server. Open and run.
🎛️
30+ Parameters
Animation speed, rotation, morph, power, lighting, fog, specular, glow, saturation, contrast, FOV, pixelate, vignette — all live, no reload.
📱
Touch & Mobile
Tap anywhere to open controls. Works on iOS and Android. Responsive layout adapts to any screen size.

// Controls

Press C or tap the canvas to reveal two side panels with 30+ real-time parameters.

Left panel: Animation & Fractal settings  ·  Right panel: Camera & Color settings
